Explain what functions are and why they are useful

A function is a basically a way of grouping a set of instructions in to a single command. Calling back to my example of washing dishes. I will have to perform this function multiple times so rather than write out the set of instructions over and over again I can just make a function called “washDishes” then IF aPlate is dirty I will washDishes until I have met the criteria that the dish is clean.
